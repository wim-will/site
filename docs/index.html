WiM WILL 2021

Overview

Women in MICCAI (WiM) is happy to announce the new WiM Inspirational Leadership Legacy (WILL) initiative with a goal to enhance and spotlight our leaders and to build our community legacy. WiM-WILL aims at providing a digital platform where MICCAI members can share their career pathways as well as building a playlist/database of our community that will facilitate in providing a bigger picture of our community to the outside world. Participants will share video interviews with inspirational leaders (e.g., supervisor, colleague, young researchers (including Ph.D students), etc) in their fields. An anonymous panel of judges will first review and shortlist the best videos based on the content and interviewerâ€™s skills. The top 3 videos will be selected following a public vote and each of the 3 winning teams will receive an award of $300. All competing videos will be made public via the WiM WILL YouTube channel.

Motivation

MICCAI community is formed of exceptional leaders which can be exemplars to the students considering a career in medical imaging and computer-assisted intervention. WiM-WILL will provide a digital platform where MICCAI members can share their career pathways. With the WILL initiative, our plan is to build a playlist/database of our community which will not only promote MICCAI, but will also facilitate providing a bigger picture of our community to the outside world.

Diversity Statement

We promote and value diversity and inclusiveness in the MICCAI community. The WILL initiative welcomes participants, regardless of age, gender, identity, race, and ethnicity, for both the interviewer(s) and the interviewee.
