Guidelines/Rules

  • The program is open to everyone regardless of age, gender, identity, race, ethnicity, and career stage (students, postdocs, early career researchers, academics) for both the interviewer(s) and the interviewee
  • An interviewee can be a person at any career stage (including young researchers and Ph.D students) with inspirational contributions to the MICCAI research fields and society.
  • The interviewee should be only one person.
  • Participants are allowed to make a team of up to 2 members (i.e., interviewers) who will work together towards preparing and conducting an interview with their inspirational interviewee.
  • Participating individual/team can make no more than two submissions.
  • Each submission should be no less than 10 minutes in duration. Maximum allowed duration is 15 minutes.
  • We encourage participants to be registered for MICCAI Conference but this is not mandatory.
  • The awards will only be for the participating team and not for the interviewee.

Evaluation Criteria


Aspects to cover in the interview (but not limited to)

  • Advice from the heart that the interviewee gives to our research community members (a take-away story maybe).
  • Career trajectories, shifts (from academia to industry or vice versa), and development.
  • Stories on how the interviewee joined the medical imaging/machine learning community and what inspires them to continue in this field.
  • The potential in medical imaging research for newbies.
  • The challenges faced at the beginning of their careers, and how it turned to be a motivational story (whether personal or career-wise).
  • Sharing success stories/struggles with graduate students and post-docs on publishing manuscripts and/or grant writing.
  • Entrepreneurial/Startups challenges that are most commonly faced and tips to overcome them.

Interviewer skills

  • Confidence
  • Preparation
  • HD video and Audio Quality
  • Interview content
  • Interview flow

Technical Requirements

  • For online interviews, we encourage the participants to use a high bandwidth internet connection to ensure high quality media recording.
