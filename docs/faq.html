➀ Who can participate?


✔ For both interviewer and interviewee: all are welcome to participate regardless of their age, gender, identity, race, ethnicity and academic position.

✔ We encourage participants to be registered for MICCAI Conference but this is not mandatory.

➁ What can I talk about?


✔ If you have any inspiring stories to share with our MICCAI community regarding your research trajectory, challenges you have met and how you solved them, publishing at MICCA, etc, the WILL is for you!

➂ I am still a student, can I be an interviewee?


✔ Yes. You have inspiring tips or stories to share, the WILL is for you too!
